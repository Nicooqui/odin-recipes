How to make an argentinian Choripan

Description

This is easy top #1 in my ranking of argentinian dishes

We are going to teach you in a few steps how to cook this delicious traditional dish

Ingredients

  • 4 chorizos
  • 4 baguettes

Steps

  1. Add coal to the grill
  2. Cook the chorizos 20 minutes. You could use a fork to make little holes in them.
  3. Make sure it cooks both sides
  4. "Chorizo Mariposa": it's when you cut the chorizos in a vertical way.
  5. Open the baguettes in half, put them in the grill and make sure they are crusty
  6. PLace the chorizos in the crusty baguette and enjoy !
